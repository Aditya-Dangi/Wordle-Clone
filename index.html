Wordle

Objective: Guess the hidden five-letter word within six attempts.

Rules:

  • You have 6 attempts to guess the word.
  • Each word must be a valid English word of exactly 5 letters.
  • After each guess, feedback will be provided:
    • Green: Correct letter in the correct position.
    • Yellow: Correct letter, wrong position.
    • Red: Incorrect letter.
  • Win by guessing the word in 6 attempts or less.
